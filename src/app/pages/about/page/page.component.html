Welcome to the Surveillance Health Information Exchange Linkage Data (SHIELD) Module!

The Surveillance Health Information Exchange Linkage Data (SHIELD) system is a centralized data repository developed by the Ministry of Health (MOH) to facilitate easy access to disease surveillance data and to enhance public health planning and response.

Overview

SHIELD aims to seamlessly integrate multiple heterogeneous health data sources, such as the Kenya Health Information System (KHIS-2), Electronic Health Records (EHRs), and Laboratory Information Management Systems (LIMS), to create a comprehensive platform for accessing surveillance data on prioritized diseases and syndromes according to the Kenya Disease Surveillance Guidelines.

Data-driven

To support timely and effective data use, the data analyzed in SHIELD is visualized through dashboards for routine surveillance, event-based surveillance, outbreak response, and program management, enabling data-driven decision-making. Additionally, SHIELD supports and enables real-time disease monitoring capabilities.

Objective

SHIELD aims to centralize all data of surveillance value into a unified data resource, allowing public health officials to monitor all health data proactively to facilitate early disease detection, understand the magnitude of health threats, and help guide responses to emerging and re-emerging disease outbreaks.
